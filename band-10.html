One of the best cover bands in London & Essex today

There is probably no one that hasn’t heard of East of Java. Comprising of hugely experienced professional musicians, each successful in their own right. In the many years since they formed, they have developed a huge following of loyal fans and have risen to the very top of London & Essex’s live music circuit. Having completed many tours in Europe as well as a special appearance in the USA, you know you’ll be in for a great treat.
