 More info

  • Payment is by cash or card, on the door.
  • Entrance is free for CAMRA members at all sessions upon producing a valid membership card.
  • Not a CAMRA member? You can join CAMRA online or at the festival.
  • Due to fire regulations, there is a limit to the number of people allowed on site. When busy, we may have to restrict entrance.
  • There is a refundable £3 deposit on all festival glasses, which you can keep as a souvenir. The refund is only available if the glass is not damaged and the refund counter is open.

 Age Verification

Under 18s are allowed on site, but must be with a responsible adult – a 17 year old with an 18 or 19 year old friend is not sufficient. As at any licensed premises, it is illegal to buy alcohol if you are under 18, or to buy alcohol for anyone under 18.

The festival supports and operates the Challenge 21 scheme; if you are lucky enough to appear under 21, then please don’t be offended if you are asked for ID to prove you are over 18. The only acceptable ID is a government issued photo ID (photo driving licence or passport) or a card showing the PASS Scheme hologram. After your ID has been checked you will be issued with a wrist band to confirm that you are over 18. We thank you, in advance, for your cooperation and understanding.

 Card Payments

We accept card and contactless payments at our entrance only. You cannot pay with card or contactless at the bars, however you can request cashback (£50 limit) when paying at the entrance or by going to the Membership & Information stand.

 Accessibility

While the festival is outdoors, disabled access is possible.
