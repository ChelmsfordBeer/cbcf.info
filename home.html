Chelmsford Winter Beer & Cider Festival 2019

Wednesday 20th February to Saturday 23rd February

Open from 12pm each day

King Edward VI Grammar School (KEGS), Broomfield Road, CM1 3SX

  • 170+ Real Ales
  • 50+ Real Ciders, Perries & Pyders
  • Hot & cold food and soft drinks available at all times
  • Free entrance before 6pm and then £3 after 6pm (except CAMRA members)
  • £3 refundable glass hire

Food & Snacks

Hopleaf

Great Beer deserves great food. Hopleaf are delighted to be back at the Winter Beer Festival. We use local suppliers and fresh ingredients to create a variety of homemade dishes.

  • Authentic curries from a mild Korma to fiery Naga, with sides including samosa.
  • Hand carved salt beef marinated by us to our own recipe.
  • Butcher’s sausages in Salt n Pepper French stick.
  • Locally made burgers with butter fried onions & optional homemade chilli relish.
  • Great selection of vegetarian dishes
  • Pulled pork roll with stuffing and smoky BBQ sauce
  • Ham cooked in real cider with egg and chips
  • Daily specials

    • Come for the beer, stay for the food!
    Like us on Facebook for menu updates or check on the blackboard during the festival. Allergy advice available on request.

    Oddfellows Chocolate

    Oddfellows (formerly Merry Berry) oddfellowschocolate.co.uk will have a range of truffles and chocolates.
